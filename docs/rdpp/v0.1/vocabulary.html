This document describes the Railway Digital Product Passport Vocabulary, i.e., the Vocabulary for Digital Product Passports (DPP) in the railway steel domain, aligned with the DPP System Standard semantic and functional data model and EN 13674-1 for rail steel acceptance. .

Alternate versions of the vocabulary definition exist in Turtle and JSON-LD.

Published:
2026-02-11
Version Info:
0.1
See Also:

This vocabulary has been defined by the railway dpp project and provides an experimental RDFS vocabulary for the Electronic Attestation of Attributes of the railway dpp project. Comments regarding this document are welcome. Please file issues directly on GitHub, or send them to tbd.

Term definitions

Property definitions

The following are property definitions in the rddp namespace.

versionOfStandard

Version of Standard

List of standards and versions applied to define product properties and documentation. This aligns with the repository and dictionary model of the DPP System Standard, where property templates are defined within standards and units are controlled through shared vocabularies.
Domain:
ProductGroup
Range:
xsd:string

In a JSON-LD representation, values to this property are supposed to be represented in the form of a JSON array, even if there is just a single value.

Relevant @context:
https://railway-dpp.github.io/vocabulary/rdpp-context/v0.1

railProfile

Rail Profile

Domain-specific product property identifying the rail profile. It is modeled as a single-valued product property within the Product Property Collection of the DPP System Standard.
Domain:
ProductGroup
Range:
xsd:string
Relevant @context:
https://railway-dpp.github.io/vocabulary/rdpp-context/v0.1

steelGrade

Domain-specific product property indicating the steel grade. Mechanical conformity is validated through tensile strength and elongation testing procedures defined in EN 13674-1, clause 8.7, including predictive equations, periodic testing, and corrective actions.

xsd:string
Relevant @context:
https://railway-dpp.github.io/vocabulary/rdpp-context/v0.1

Class definitions

The following are class definitions in the rddp namespace.

CredentialSubject

Credential Subject

See also the formal definition of the term.

This term is formally defined in another vocabulary (as cred:CredentialSubject), but is frequently used with this vocabulary and has been included to aid readability of this document.

ProductGroup

Product Group

Groups rail products that share essential technical characteristics (such as rail profile and steel grade) so they can be managed and assessed consistently within a Digital Product Passport. In the DPP System Standard semantic model, such characteristics are represented as product properties within a Product Property Collection, while related certificates and documents are modeled as information products. Steel grade-related acceptance and mechanical conformity are validated through tensile strength and elongation testing procedures defined in EN 13674-1.
Subclass of:
CredentialSubject
Domain of:
versionOfStandard, railProfile, steelGrade, economicOperatorId
Relevant @context:
https://railway-dpp.github.io/vocabulary/rdpp-context/v0.1

Transport

Transport

Models a transport credential subject capturing responsible service provider, time-bound movement data, and environmental impact metrics. Transport aligns with the transactional model’s business steps (e.g., shipping) and event times, and service providers are represented as stakeholder roles within the DPP ecosystem.
Subclass of:
CredentialSubject
Domain of:
serviceProviderId, timeDeparture, timeArrival, globalWarmingPotentialTransport, transportBatchId
Relevant @context:
https://railway-dpp.github.io/vocabulary/rdpp-context/v0.1

TransportBatch

Transport Batch

Represents a shipment batch aggregating one or more transport events for a given DPP. It captures origin and destination to support traceability across movements and aligns with the data model’s identification pattern for data elements.
Subclass of:
CredentialSubject
Domain of:
locationDeparture, locationArrival, dppId
Relevant @context:
https://railway-dpp.github.io/vocabulary/rdpp-context/v0.1

DPP

Digital Product Passport

The Digital Product Passport acts as the digital container for product metadata, product properties, and lifecycle-related information. Core metadata includes identifiers, product name, granularity, and facility references, while properties such as length and weight are modeled as data elements with controlled units.
Subclass of:
CredentialSubject
Domain of:
productId, name, granularity, facilityId, length, weight, dppSchemaId
In the domain of:
productGroupId
Relevant @context:
https://railway-dpp.github.io/vocabulary/rdpp-context/v0.1

EUDPPRegistry

EU DPP Registry

Represents the EU Digital Product Passport Registry, where DPPs are registered to support discovery, conformity assessment, and market surveillance. Registration is an interoperability-relevant moment and a responsibility of economic operators.
Domain of:
dppRegistrationNumber
Relevant @context:
https://railway-dpp.github.io/vocabulary/rdpp-context/v0.1

DppSchema

DPP Schema

Captures the schema or profile used by DPP instances. In the DPP System Standard model, schemas reference open repositories containing property templates, standards, and controlled units, while data elements carry dictionary references.
Domain of:
version, templateName
Relevant @context:
https://railway-dpp.github.io/vocabulary/rdpp-context/v0.1

DoPC

Declaration of Product Conformity

Declaration of Product Conformity modeled as a credential subject. In the DPP semantic model, such declarations are treated as information products with metadata (issuer, issue date) and references to applicable standards.
Subclass of:
CredentialSubject
Domain of:
deglerationOfProductConformity, epdId
Relevant @context:
https://railway-dpp.github.io/vocabulary/rdpp-context/v0.1

EPD Production
